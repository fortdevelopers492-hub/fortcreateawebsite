Step 1: Read and agree to the terms and conditions and privacy policy

This is to avoid any form of disagreement and misunderstanding in the future.

Step 2: Provide any Necessary Information

This will be done by pressing the create website button or website creation button and therefore proceeding to the info page. Here one will provide the necessary info needed and describe all the features of the websites as simply as possible to avoid any misunderstanding. Info provided will range from theme, colour pallates, features, pages, layout etc. Templates can also be provided for you to choose from if needed.

Step 3: Sending to Fort

Click the send to fort button after filling out necessary info. This will prompt a modal where you should input your valid email address asd your passwrd. Please always provide a valid email address owed by you. Then after the email have been sent click the send button in the whatsapp modal that pops up to send your files via Whatsapp manually.

Step 4: Specification of Cost

After the above stages have been done, the next step will be specification of cost by the developer. Here based on the complexity, specifications and degree of customization of the website the price will be provided with little room for negotiation. This specification of cost will be done after website info has been checked and properly gone through by the developer.
